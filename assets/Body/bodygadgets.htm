Mobiles

Samsung SGH-E900

 One of my most favourite phones of all time. I have love how the interface looks. I also love how there is a Newton's Cradle animation on the timer. My specific device runs on Orange UK, and has it's own Orange branded firmware and rear cover. This is my main device, and I love it. I am not going to upgrade to those huge touch screen phones that are all the rage now. Just another gimmick that will quickly die out in my opinion.

Release: 2006

Nokia 1600

 My first ever phone. The battery life is great on it, and to this day, it still lasts a week on a single charge. The phone runs on the T-Mobile UK network, and it too has T-Mobile branding on the back. I remember having (horribly) made the Half-Life 2 song "Triage At Dawn" on the composer. I like the screen savers it has too.

Release: 2005

NEC e313

 Commonly referred by me as the "3 Phone", The NEC e313 was the "in-between" phone I used after the 1600's charger broke. It was a phone that was sitting in a box for years. It ran on the carrier network known as 3. I remember finding the interface very tedious, especially with it's loud beeps.

Release: 2004
