About Axl ...Just Who The Heck Am I?

Hello! I'm Axl, I am a furry in the UK who is mostly self taught in the creative arts sector. I have taught myself in Art the most, but I've also taught myself some music stuff. I have a mad love for retro tech, and even made this website on a retro machine!

I'm also a little bit of a gamer, but not that much. I play such games such as Nintendo's Star Fox, 3D Realms' Duke Nukem/Terminal Velocity, ID's Doom, Squaresoft's Final Fantasy VII and Valve's Half Life! Did you know that Star Fox was the main catalyst for my love of fighter aircrafts and spacecraft? It's been my dream for a long while to fly my own ship like the Arwing.

In terms of Art, I've been drawing for almost as long as I have been alive (Not saying it was far from a scribble though!) At around 2018, I was bullied quite a lot in school, which induced a depression which led to me stopping art, and destroying EVERYTHING that I've done. Only a few artworks from this time survived.

A few years later, just before lockdown, I worked up the courage to begin doing art again, but under the fluffy alias you see now! Since my art skills were lost over time, I decided to just use the game "Garry's Mod" to pose Barney Calhoun. This was my reference for poses. This would continue for a year or two until my laptop broke. This stopped me from doing art for 2 months, as one of the main pillars was just swept from underneath. After these 2 months, I eventually worked up the courage to learn to stand without this pillar, and I learned to pose without the help of Garry's Mod. The proportions at this time still were not great, and they were quite skinny and pointy, but I kept going, slowly improving my skills overtime. Some other artists helped me learn stuff in recent times too! Great thanks to them!

In recent times, I have also been getting into story writing. Most of my artworks come with stories. People seem to really enjoy the stories I put along with artworks. Below is a snippet of a full story I am working on!

While I'm no music professional, I do rarely like to do it as a side hobby. While most of my works are just mixes of readily made samples, I do sometimes come up with some original compositions. If you want an example, scroll down to the bottom of this page and press the "Credits" button. A song that I made myself should begin playing. This song was for a long scrapped animation project of mine. You can find a W.I.P of where specifically the song was used on my YouTube channel.

In terms of retro tech, I am one hell of a nerd. I have collected several retro phones. I'll be honest, I don't believe in something becoming obsolete. My main phone is still a Samsung SGH-E900 from 2007. I've been able to rely on it much more than my more modern phone. I have even gotten to grips with the Apple ][ and Commodore 64 too. I have previously made a very basic password system for both machines. The very machine I made this website on was built in 2004, and was the old family PC. It was fully dead and I spent a few years reviving it. Going back to the obsolete disbelief, I still use all sorts of "obsolete" things to this day! I daily drive and am able to rely on the E900, and the Fujitsu Siemens SCALEO P, to this day they still work great. Did you know that I still use floppy disks too? My Dell Inspiron 1545 has actually gotten me through my work while modern tech failed. I have also proven just how capable "obsolete" tech can still be. I have been able to make art on the Apple Macintosh and Windows 1.01!

I also have some knowledge on making maps in the Source Engine (The engine Half-Life 2, Team Fortress 2 and Garry's Mod run on). I have been working to make a local town I know as it was back in 2011, another local town from 2009, a hangout map, and a Mario Kart inspired track.

Occasionally, I also get into making neat little scripts. For instance, I had made a script file that randomly chooses a character of mine for use in upcoming artworks! It's rather simple, but it works!

I also love to preserve things. In recent years, I have specifically taken interest into TV archival. I have gathered up a collection of old ITV and BBC content. A lot of my research and data is good enough for you to go ahead and make your own mock stuff! This ranges from show themes (Such as ITV's GMTV and Daybreak) to Idents, their full audio, and their continuities! This is probably fuelled by my love for retro stuff.

Thanks for being interested in what I do, and thanks for reading!
