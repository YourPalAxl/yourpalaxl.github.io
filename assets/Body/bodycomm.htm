Commission Policy

Warning:

If you would like to fill in a slot, please take heed of the status. If you feel doubts on whether this has been updated, please do not be afraid to message me by any of the given methods. Please refrain from asking said question if the last update date is recent (Below a month). Please also refrain from nagging for the status (Constant requests for the status in quick succession). Doing so may get you temporarily suspended from taking any slots in the future.

Please do keep in mind that I:

A: Reserve the right to draw gift art for people.

I do this out of the goodness of my own heart. Please do not request a gift.

B: Reserve the right to cancel any Commissions, Trades*, or requests.

If I cancel a commission, I will refund the money or product, if possible. If not, I will try my best to find an equivalent, or will try to work on what you wanted at a later time. I will also try to refund if you decide to back out**.
*If your end of a trade has been done, to prevent accidental scamming, I will always try to do it at a later date.
**There will not be refunds if the artwork gets completed. Any attempt to request a refund after this may come across as a scam attempt. You may then be indefinitely suspended from future slot openings.

C: Reserve the right to deny anything at anytime.

I am obliged to deny any requests, trades, or commissions at any time. This has a higher chance of occurring if:

  • You attempt to make a request, trade, or commission during the time there are no slots open.

  • What you wanted fails to comply by certain standards (mentioned further down)

  • If there are concurrent hardware issues (Such as computer failures)

  • You are suspended from taking any slots

  • You try to do a payment without authorisation.

If I do deny your request, I will try to give a reason as to why. If the reason is due to what you want not falling under standards, I will give you another chance to have something else.

D: Suspend people from taking slots, whether it be temporary, or indefinitely

I will not allow people who are suspended to be able to fill a spot. This could happen to you if:

  • You have any sort of repeated attitude

  • What you want constantly fails to fall under standards. I will notice if this is accidental, which will be an exception

  • You try to force yourself into the queue. This will result in an immediate and indefinite suspension.

  • Repeated attempts to pay without authorisation.

E: Contact the relevant authorities If your description induces extreme concern, or is not acceptable by law.

I am always allowed to contact the relevant authorities should what you want cause me to question your safety, or the safety of others.

I will also do it should your request fall under unlawful terms.

Working Days

To help prevent me from getting overworked I may only work on agreed commissions, trades and requests on certain days. Outside of these working days, I will take a break until the new "week" begins. Only personal art will be worked on at this time. It will also help contain my on-going distraction issues.

These working days are:

  • Wednesday-Friday: May be Inconsistent. Depends on the day. (College)

  • Monday and Tuesday: I will work on them if I have the energy.

  • Saturday and Sunday: Personal art only.
