Character Index ...Want to get to know everyone, eh?

Drawings of characters are coming soon...

Axl

Axl is a wolf who lives on the planet Viona with who he now considers family. He has a magical tail that can grow to vast sizes! He can also pull of manoeuvres known to be completely impossible by human physics teachings. It is unknown how Axl was created, but few believe he was the result of illegal experimentation by humans (Using Vionan blood), before he was saved and transported to Viona by Justine. Axl is the leader of the Nightfire Squadron. Unlike most normal wolves, Axl can actually purr, very loudly too...

Justine

 Justine is a Vionan cat who really likes adventure, more specifically "Free-running". Justine also has very contagious happiness, if you come to her upset, she will not leave you alone, and you'll be happy again in no time! Justine has mutually considered Axl family ever since the day she plucked him from Earth. Justine also has the ability to control and create water (though a limited supply). Justine is also a member of the Nightfire Squadron. She's the glue that holds the team together.

Volt

 Volt is a Vionan lynx, he is great friends with Cross. He helped with the operation of taking Axl from Earth. He is able to create electricity for a short while. That and the bolt-like patterns on his fur had given him the nickname "Volt". He seems to like the Volt name more than he does his real name. Volt is also a member of the Nightfire Squadron. He serves as the brains of the team.

Felicity

 Felicity is a hotshot aerial nerd with a love for all things aircraft of times past. She always tries to act tough but even a simple hair ruffle will make the walls come crashing down. While she can't control her powers, she has, on some occasions of extreme tension, managed to heat up her fur, create small fireballs and some sparks. She is also a member of the Nightfire Squadron, and she serves as the muscle of the team.

Spirit

 Spirit is a Vionan fox who is exceptionally good at all things science. She helped Axl in his recovery after leaving Earth. She is great friends with Fang, and tends to work with Volt, Cross and the Nightfire Squadron. In her spare time though, she tends to make living plushies that are twice your size. If you go home and suddenly get tackled to the floor and cuddled by a plush version your favourite character, it's likely Spirit's doing.

Fang

 Fang is a Vionan Wolf with a love for all things action. He loves making motorcycles, and even designed and made one for Justine. Don't let his look intimidate you, he's actually very kind. He does like to be the lone wolf though. He's great friends with Spirit, and visits her in the lab a lot.

[CROSS]

 Cross is a Vionan wolf who specialises in all things science. He was one of several responsible for the operation to retrieve Axl from Earth. He, along with Justine, had taken care of Axl for a while, and taught him all about Viona. He has no known powers. He is part of the family that includes Axl and Justine.

Forest

 Forest is a chill and laid-back Vionan fox. Things are always calm around her. She has no known superpowers. She has a very calming aura. She is a close friend of Axl and Justine.

Snow

 An inflatable statue of a mascot from a completely forgotten and obscure ski resort, she woken up, and was completely unsure of her origin. She is however very kind. She restored and now runs the ski resort, the resort is now the most popular in Viona. She lives in the snowy hills just near the resort. She will always help anyone in need, and will even bring them into her house if needed. This is how she met Axl. To this day, nobody knows of her origin. Her jacket is something magical too. She can duplicate it and put it on someone else. This heats or cools the wearer to the just right temperature, no matter how cold, or hot.

Lavender

 

 Lavender is a friendly goo creature even more of a mystery than Mallow. Her goo has a scent of lavender strong enough to send even the most energetic of people to sleep quickly. Her goo can easily reform into another shape, or even harden. Under the right conditions, her goo can even form a very strong charge. Despite all this, if her goo ends up on you, or your clothes, it will come straight off, leaving zero residue. She lives in a building that was built by Spirit at the bottom of a hill. She will always help anyone in need of some rest.

[MALLOW]

 Mallow is one of the more peculiar characters. Mallow is formed entirely out of marshmallow goo. It is unknown how she is able to retain her form. She is actually very friendly, and will hug. A lot... Good luck getting it out of your fur and clothes. Mallow always takes on the form of a wolf. Her and Justine once together made an incredible hot chocolate, that people adore. So now it's become a tradition among them and their friends.

[WINTER]

 Winter is a living pooltoy husky that Justine ordered from a suspicious website. Originally designed to just be a thing for cuddling, Axl somehow managed to give her life after finding a collar in the box. She became one of Axl's friends, helping him when it came to anything. She is a very understanding and cuddly being.

[FENNIX]

 Neither from Earth nor Viona, Her species is unknown, but Axl calls her species a "Snow-Wolf". This is due to her looking like a mix between a snow leopard and a wolf. Her goo is able to give others the same appearance as her. The process remains a mystery, but can easily be reversed by members of her species (If you decide to reverse in under a month since the initial transformation. If it's more than a month, it will be  permanent.) She is very kind though, and will not do anything without request.

[VIOLET]

 Violet is a Vionan Wolf. She is known for her amazing stealth capabilities. She is similar to Axl, but can become a lot more unstable. Her and Axl didn't get a long at first, but they learned to get along. Violet also owns a husky, rescued from Earth.

[VIXEN]

 Vixen is a goo creature of unknown origin. Thought as an off-planet creature, as he contains zero elements on the Vionan or Terran (Earth) periodic tables. Not much is known about him, except he lives in an abandoned lab, giving hospitality to those who pass by.

[MIDNIGHT]

 The prototype to Iris, Midnight is a robotic character repurposed for helping people get tedious jobs done. She also has built in roller skates, able to match normal cars in speed. Somehow, she's road legal.

[IRIS]

 The final product built off of Midnight. Iris is a hazmat suit robot. She can protect the wearer from almost all kinds of radiation. Her large tail also has a storage compartment big enough to put a person inside. She too can roller skate.

[NOELLE]

 Noelle is exactly like Snow, and was the main antagonist mascot for the abandoned ski resort. She became friends with Snow, and they both run the resort together, she lives next door to Snow.

[KAT]

 Kat is a feline witch who used to only appear on the day of Halloween. Every Halloween she would pull harmless pranks and cause harmless trouble for anyone she saw. She may also pull someone along for the ride. At the end of the night, she'd cuddle this person under a specific tree. The person would then wake up back in their own bed. The curse was somehow lifted when she dragged Axl along. Kat and Axl are now great friends, though it doesn't stop her from causing Axl harmless trouble.

[PEPPERMINT]

 For the sake of convenience, people tend to call her a snow leopard, while that is true, she does bear a lot of differences from a normal snow leopard.

Her tentacles can be used to inject her own goo into someone. Small intakes can cause extreme tiredness, or extreme disorientation, tiny intakes may cause the person to be sent into a relaxed state. larger intakes can cause paralysis, or it can prevent movement in your body entirely. She tends to be lenient on her venomous abilities. She will commonly let the few she considers friends snuggle up with her giant tail.

[FLORA]

 Flora was originally a simple lumavian slime blob. However, it learned to gain and maintain a humanoid form. Flora has since learned to help visitors and residents of Lumavia. She's helped members of the Nightfire Squadron on several occasions.

[ZERO]

 Zero is a character that was originally intended for use in my never finished Source Engine title "Echoes Of Dawn". Currently I still need to think his character up more...

[INDIGO]

 Indigo is also a character that was intended for my scrapped Source Engine title "Echoes Of Dawn". She is now a great friend of Justine and Axl. She works in a Vionan high street, where she makes the softest blankets (and closely related things) you could ever ask for. Her stuff is that much better than human counterparts, that she has to keep a warning on her storefront for any human customers.

[ROSE]

 Rose is a rose coloured wolf with a signature rose clip in her hair. She may commonly intrude on missions the Nightfire Squadron undertake and help them. Even if help isn't actually needed.

[CUDDLES]

 Cuddles is the first plushie fur suit robot designed by Spirit. He was designed specifically with the purpose of helping people get some better rest. His interior also features holographic technology, that takes the wearer to an environment where they feel safe.

[SNUGGLES]

 Snuggles is the second plushie fur suit robot designed by Spirit. She is commonly seen teaming up with Cuddles to send their targets to sleep. Their most common method is getting you caught between them like a sandwich. From there, they'll endlessly cuddle until you fall asleep. Unlike Cuddles, Snuggles sports two thick wings with a similar material to that of a sleeping bag. They are not for flying however. If she manages to get you. She will wrap you up in these wings and endlessley cuddle you. That's if she doesn't choose to stuff you inside.

[CRYSTAL]

 Crystal is a lumavian wolf composed of a teal slime. She is believed to have emerged from pool of crystal clear water. Unlike the other slimes you know of, she is larger than them. When the Nightfire Squadron visit Lumavia, she tends to wait until they finish their mission, where she'll suddenly grab and flee with 1 or 2 of the members. She'll then take them for a ride around the area whilst keeping them safe from harm.

[GENERAL WOLFE]

 Wolfe used to be a great general for the Vionan United Military. However, when his wife's life was taken by a human wannabe gangster, Wolfe swore revenge. Ever since, he's been trying to end humanity and destroy the Earth in her name. No matter the cost, be it Vionan or Human.
